Focal distance :
The image is displayed in the startpage and offers an overview of the measurement. The
camera must then be aligned so that 2 distinct image points (focus) are visible on the
screen.

Pressing the Pause/Play button then starts the measurment. The pixels with the highest
light intensity are detected and each beam is located. The distance between the beams
is then calculated.
